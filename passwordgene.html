What makes a password safe?

Key Elements of a Strong Password

A safe password is strong, unique, and difficult to guess. The key characteristics of a secure password include sufficient length (at least 12-16 characters) and a random combination of uppercase and lowercase letters, numbers, and special symbols. Avoiding common words, predictable patterns, and personal information, like birthdays or names—further strengthens its security.
